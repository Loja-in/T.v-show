Season 1 (2004)

The first season of House M.D., which aired from September 5 to December 9, 2004 on Fox, introduces the main characters and sets the stage for future episodes. It follows Dr. Gregory House (played by Hugh Laurie), a brilliant but eccentric doctor who leads an interdisciplinary team at San Francisco General Hospital. The cast also includes Robert Sean Leonard as Dr. Eric Foreman, Lisa Cuddy (Cameron Britton), David Shryb (Dr. James "Tag" Walker), Olivia Wilde as Dr. Lisa Nicole "Lisa" Neeson, and Amber Guilbert as Dr. Allison "Allie Bailey".
